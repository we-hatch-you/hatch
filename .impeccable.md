### Aesthetic Direction
A highly premium, pristine white canvas heavily inspired by modern fintech (e.g., Stripe, CRED, Slice). Key characteristics include:
- Typography: 'Plus Jakarta Sans' for a sharp, clean, geometric feel.
- Colors: Soft, organic mesh gradients featuring 'Hatch Orange' (#EE6C4D) and 'Hatch Peach' (#F4A261) against warm off-white backgrounds (#FDFCFB).
- Texture: Extensive use of glassmorphism (frosted glass) and highly refined drop shadows.
- Animations: Smooth, 60fps Framer Motion reveals with slight 3D tilts and completely fluid, lag-free CSS radial gradient backgrounds.
